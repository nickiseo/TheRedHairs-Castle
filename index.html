The Redhairs

Great Chain of humans, animals, robots and mutant viruses living in the contemporay world.
the title of 'The Great Chain of Being' is taken from an ancient philosophical concept, which explains the world as a type of hierarchical structure.
The virtual consists of robots, machines, humans, the stages of creation, allocation, manipulation, experimentation, disposal, and recycling. And they repeatedly appear and disappear, collide and defend each other.

Sungrok Choi (born in Goyang, 1978, lives and works in Seoul) has engaged in digital animation and video work to explore contemporary landscapes and narratives generated by techmology.
He is interested in understanding how the relationships change due to technological advancement, human experimence, and new senses that expand as a result, and continues to explore how human beings are perceived in the context of existence in the digitized word.
