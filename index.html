The Redhairs

The redhairs' castle, animals, robots and mutant viruses living in the contemporay world.
the title of 'The redhair' is taken from an ancient philosophical concept, which explains the world as a type of hierarchical structure.
The virtual consists of robots, machines, humans, the stages of creation, allocation, manipulation, experimentation, disposal, and recycling. And they repeatedly appear and disappear, collide and defend each other.

Hyojung Nicki Seo (born in Seoul, 1997, lives and works in Toronto) has engaged in digital animation and video work to explore contemporary landscapes and narratives generated by technology.
she is interested in understanding how the relationships change due to technological advancement, human experimence, and new senses that expand as a result, and continues to explore how human beings are perceived in the context of existence in the digitized word.
